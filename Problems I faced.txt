The Base Image is not compatible for AWS LAMBDA the code for Dockerfile is different for AWS Lambda and for local machine.

For AWS Lambda:
FROM public.ecr.aws/lambda/python:3.11

For Local Machine:
FROM python:3.11-slim

The Waiter (Lambda) is only allowed to stay at your table for a few seconds.
SSE Streaming is like the waiter bringing you one spoonful of soup every 5 seconds and staying at your table until the whole bowl is finished.
